This Website:

This website started as a side project during my summer 2023 internship at New England Biolabs. Whenever I wasn't looking at spreadsheets, I would learn HTML and edit the site.


The concept of me having a website was pretty much ignored through the later half of 2023 and and mid-2024 when my domain expired and I never bothered to renew it. If you can read this, that means I got the domain back.
Looking again at the website after seeing multiple examples of others' personal sites, I was pretty dissatisfied with how it looked. Since it had also been so long since I worked on the website, most of my work was imcomprehensible to me. I decided the best solution was to start from scratch with what I did know. So far, I already like it a lot better.
